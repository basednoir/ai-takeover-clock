About This Clock & Data

The AI Takeover Clock provides dynamic estimates intended to illustrate the potential scale and pace of AI's global impact. The numbers displayed are calculated using a time-based model beginning January 1st, 2025.

Rates and benchmarks used in these calculations are derived from analyzing publicly available research, forecasts, and reports from reputable technology analysis groups, economic organizations, and intellectual property trackers.

Please note these figures are illustrative estimates and simulations, not precise real-time data feeds. Data for metrics like AI-Generated Content and the Ethical Concern level involve significant estimation due to inherent quantification challenges. The methodology and underlying estimates may be updated periodically based on new research.
